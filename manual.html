Introduction & Concept

Purpose of the System

The Reception Management System is a dual-platform bridge designed to connect field receptionists directly with superior officers and administrative command.

Web Portal: Used for oversight, advanced reporting, and official resolution logging.
Android App: Used for real-time data entry at police stations and mobile oversight for superiors.

Core Philosophy

Records are registered at the source (PS), monitored on the move (Mobile), and closed officially via the command center (Web).

03

Access Control & Roles

Every user is assigned a specific role which dictates what they can see and what actions they can perform.

01
Super Admin Global Architect

Manages the entire subdivision. Can change the site name, update logos, add/remove police stations, and define the standard "Action Taken" types for everyone.

02
Admin Station Administrator

Manages users and category types. They ensure the correct staff are assigned to the correct police stations.

03
Superior (CP/SP/IC) Operational Oversight

The "Reviewers." They receive alerts for high-priority cases. They use the mobile app to give instructions and the web portal to log official resolutions.

04
Receptionist Data Entry Specialist

Front-line staff. They exclusively use the mobile app to enter complainant data as they walk in. They cannot see records from other stations.

04

Field Operations: Receptionist

How to register a complaint:

1
Open New Complain

Tap the 'Add' button on the mobile home screen.

2
Enter Citizen Details

Input Name, Mobile Number, and Address accurately. These are vital for tracking.

3
Select Category

Choose the correct type. Note: Categories marked 'High Priority' will trigger an immediate alert to Superiors.

Important: Ensure the phone number is verified before saving. Direct calling depends on this field.
App: New Complain Entry Screen
05

Mobile Oversight: Superior Officers

App: Superior Note Input Screen

Reviewing on the go:

Superior officers (CP/SP/IC) have a specialized dashboard in the app to monitor their jurisdiction.

1
Receive Alert

When a High-Priority case is filed, you will receive a push notification. Tap it to open the record.

2
Add Instructions

Scroll to the bottom of the complaint detail. Enter your guidance in the "SUPERIOR NOTE" field.

3
Immediate Guidance

Tap "Save Official Note". This instruction is instantly visible to the station staff on their app and web view.

06

Web Portal: Final Resolution

Closing a Record (Official Action)

While notes are for internal guidance, "Action Taken" is for the final resolution. This can ONLY be done via the Web Portal.

1
Navigate to /complaints

Find the complaint in the list. Use the "Search" or "Sub-Category" filter if needed.

2
Update Action

Click the Yellow Pen Icon on the right side of the row.

3
Log Resolution

Select the Action (e.g., 'FIR Lodged') from the dropdown and provide specific details (e.g., 'Case No 123/24 registered').

Web: Action Taken Modal (Dropdown & Details)
07

Executive Monitoring (Dashboard)

The Web Dashboard is the "Command Center" for the subdivision.

Understanding the Metrics:

  • Total Records: Every entry ever registered.
  • Total Done: Number of complaints where "Action Taken" has been logged.
  • Sensitive Complain No Action:

    This is the most critical metric. It counts High-Priority cases that are older than 24 hours but have NO action taken. Investigate these immediately.

Pro Tip: Click any Station Card to immediately filter the complaints view for that station.
Web: Main Dashboard Cards & Pulse Indicators
08

Advanced Reporting & CSV

The system allows you to generate detailed Excel-compatible reports in seconds.

1
Apply Your Filters

Select a date range (From - To), a specific station, or a complaint type.

2
Review View

Ensure the data on the screen matches what you want to export.

3
Export CSV

Click the Green "EXPORT CSV" button. The system will download a file containing all complainant info, notes, and actions for your selected filters.

Web: Filtering Header & Export Button
09

Understanding System Alerts

Notifications keep the team synchronized. Here is what each alert means:

🚨 EMERGENCY ALERT

A high-priority complaint was just registered at a station. Superior attention required.

✅ ACTION UPDATE

An official resolution (Action Taken) was logged via the web portal. Case is moving toward closure.

📝 INSTRUCTION ALERT

A Superior has added a note via the app. Receptionists must read this for field guidance.

10

System Setup (Super Admin)

If you are a Super Admin, you control the identity of the entire system.

Customizing the Portal

Go to System Settings → Site Settings to update:

  • Site Name (Appears in App & Web titles)
  • Official Logo & Favicon (Browser icon)
  • Footer Text & Contact Info

Standardizing Actions

Go to System Settings → Action Taken.

Add the standard resolution phrases you want your officers to use (e.g., 'Warning Issued', 'Referred to SP'). This keeps the dropdown clean and data consistent.
